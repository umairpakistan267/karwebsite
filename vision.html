Guiding principles

Our Vision & Mission

Clear values and direction power every decision we make at K.A.R Holdings, from fuel retailing to food, property, and investment businesses across Western Canada.

Our Vision

To be the leading business group in Western Canada, recognized for excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We envision a future where K.A.R Holdings stands as a symbol of trust, quality, and sustainable growth, making a positive impact on the communities we serve.

Our vision extends beyond business success - we aim to create lasting value for our stakeholders, contribute to regional economic development, and build a legacy that inspires future generations of entrepreneurs in Western Canada.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional products and services across all our business divisions while maintaining the highest standards of quality, integrity, and customer service. We are committed to creating value for our customers, team members, partners, and communities through innovative solutions, responsible business practices, and continuous improvement.

We strive to build strong relationships with our stakeholders, foster a culture of excellence within our organization, and contribute positively to the economic and social development of Western Canada. Our mission is powered by our core values of integrity, excellence, community engagement, and innovation.

Strategic Goals

Sustainable Growth

Continue expanding our operations while maintaining profitability and market leadership in all sectors.

Excellence in Service

Maintain the highest standards of customer service across all our business units and touchpoints.

Team Development

Invest in our team members' growth and create an inclusive, supportive work environment.

Sustainability

Implement environmentally responsible practices and contribute to sustainable business operations.

Partnerships

Build and strengthen strategic partnerships that create mutual value and opportunities.

Community Impact

Make meaningful contributions to the communities where we operate through various initiatives.
