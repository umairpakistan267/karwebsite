Our leadership

Board Of Directors

Visionary founders and leaders who have transformed a single fuel station into a diversified business group across Western Canada.

Leadership That Shapes Our Future

Our Board of Directors brings together exceptional leaders with diverse expertise and a shared commitment to driving K.A.R Holdings toward continued success and growth.

Mr. Mohammad Khalid Aziz

Founder – KAR Group

Mr. Mohammad Khalid Aziz is a visionary entrepreneur and the esteemed founder of KAR Group. His journey is a testament to his unwavering commitment to success, innovation, and the pursuit of excellence in the business world.

Early Life and Education: Mr. Khalid Aziz’s remarkable journey began with a strong educational foundation. He pursued his education in business management from California State University Los Angeles in the USA, where he honed his entrepreneurial skills and laid the groundwork for his future endeavours.

Professional Experience before KAR: Our Founder was always an entrepreneur. He successfully started and ran two men’s clothing stores and a convenience store in California, immediately after completing his studies. Not content with this, and to achieve his ambitious dreams, he left California and came to Canada in 2001 to pursue his vision of accomplishing something great.

The Birth of KAR Group: In 2003, armed with a clear vision and an indomitable spirit, Mr. Khalid Aziz founded KAR Group in Western Canada. At the inception of the company, he embarked on a journey that would redefine the business landscape in the region.

A Humble Beginning: KAR Group’s inception was marked by a single fuel station operating under the Petro‑Canada brand at Okotoks, Alberta. With limited resources but boundless determination, Mr. Khalid Aziz set out to build an enterprise that would stand as a symbol of excellence in the industry.

The Aziz Brothers’ Partnership: A significant turning point in the company’s journey came when Mr. Rashid Aziz, Mr. Khalid Aziz’s brother, left his job in California to join him in Edmonton, Canada. This brotherly partnership brought together a wealth of talent, expertise, and a shared commitment to building something exceptional.

Unwavering Dedication and Growth: Both brothers worked tirelessly, applying their combined expertise and dedication to the company’s growth. With a focus on operational excellence, customer satisfaction, and industry best practices, they propelled KAR Group to new heights of success. Today the network spans a vast geographical area from White Horse, Yukon in the north to Virden, Manitoba in the east and Prince Rupert, BC in the west, while having operational headquarters centralized in Edmonton, Alberta.

Diversification and Expansion: Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Khalid Aziz, KAR Group diversified significantly. The company’s portfolio now includes retail management under renowned brands such as Petro‑Canada and Domo Gasoline, operation of quick‑service restaurants as a proud Pizza Pizza franchisee, development of gas stations, consultation to third‑party convenience stores, and its own convenience brand KIKIT.

Industry Recognition and Culture: KAR Group’s exceptional contributions have been recognised with prestigious awards, including the President’s Award and Associate of the Year Award from Suncor. Mr. Khalid Aziz has cultivated a family‑like corporate culture where every team member is valued, and the company’s success is celebrated collectively.

Summary: Mr. Mohammad Khalid Aziz’s entrepreneurial journey, marked by determination, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, has shaped the success of KAR Group and left an indelible mark on the business landscape of Western Canada.

Mr. Mohammad Rashid Aziz

President – KAR Group

Mr. Rashid Aziz, co‑owner of KAR Group, embodies an unwavering commitment to investing in and believing in people, which has helped him build long‑lasting relationships. The cornerstone of his philosophy is creating an environment built on trust and collaboration.

Early Life and Education: Mr. Aziz started working at a young age, taking on various jobs such as delivering newspapers and working as a door greeter for a department store. These roles helped him support himself throughout his undergraduate studies. He graduated in 1996 with a Bachelor’s degree in Manufacturing Systems Engineering from California State University, Northridge.

Shortly after graduating, he began his professional career as an Application Engineer at an electrolytic grounding company in Torrance, California. During this time he found a mentor whose journey greatly inspired him and reinforced his belief that anyone, regardless of background, can achieve their goals through dedication and hard work.

Joining KAR Group: In 2007 Mr. Aziz joined his brother Mohammad Khalid Aziz, founder of KAR Group, as a partner in Edmonton, Canada. Together, they devoted themselves entirely to the company’s success, setting ambitious goals and ensuring they were achieved through a shared vision and determination.

Under their leadership, KAR Group has grown to become one of the largest independent dealers in the retail gas station sector in Western Canada. The company’s achievements have been recognised within the Suncor network through numerous nominations and awards, highlighting their impactful contributions and excellence.

For Mr. Aziz, life is not just about personal success but also about recognising and honouring those who have provided support along the journey. He believes deeply in gratitude toward mentors and is committed to paying it forward by mentoring and guiding the next generation, completing the circle of life.
