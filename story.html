The Beginning

K.A.R Holdings was founded with a vision to transform the business landscape in Western Canada. What started as a small venture in fuel retailing has grown into a diversified business group spanning multiple industries.

Our founders, the Aziz brothers, recognized the need for quality service and innovation in the Canadian market. With determination, integrity, and a commitment to excellence, they built a business that serves communities across Western Canada.

Our Journey

2013

Foundation

K.A.R Holdings was established with the opening of our first Petro Canada gas station, marking the beginning of our journey in fuel retailing.

2015

Expansion Begins

We expanded our fuel retailing operations, opening multiple locations and establishing partnerships with leading fuel brands.

2017

Diversification

Entered the food business sector, opening our first quick service restaurant and beginning our journey into hospitality.

2019

Growth Phase

Expanded into property management and investment services, diversifying our portfolio and strengthening our market presence.

2021

Innovation

Launched consultancy services and enhanced our operations with state-of-the-art facilities including modern car wash services.

2023

Recognition & Partnerships

K.A.R Holdings strengthened its brand with new strategic partnerships and recognition from industry peers for operational excellence and community impact.

2026

Today

Operating 18+ Petro stations, 13+ Domo stations, and 6+ restaurants across Western Canada, K.A.R Holdings continues to invest in people, technology, and new locations to serve our communities better every day.
