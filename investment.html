Investment Business

Strategic acquisition and development of fuel stations and quick service restaurants across Western Canada through KAR Holdings Ltd.

Strategic Investment Excellence

KAR Holdings Ltd is a dynamic and forward-thinking investment company specializing in the strategic acquisition and development of fuel stations and quick service restaurants (QSRs) across Western Canada. With a vision firmly rooted in fostering growth, sustainability, and community enrichment, we have established ourselves as a prominent player in the region's thriving fuel and dining sectors.

Our Focus:

1. Fuel Stations: We understand the critical role fuel plays in everyday life, both for individuals and businesses. Our commitment to providing accessible, reliable, and environmentally responsible fuel stations is at the heart of our mission. We invest in and manage a diverse portfolio of fuel stations, ranging from trusted brands like Petro Canada, Esso and Domo Gasoline. Our continuous efforts to modernize and innovate within this sector ensure that our stations meet the evolving needs of our communities.

2. Quick Service Restaurants (QSRs): In parallel with our fuel station investments, we proudly own and operate a network of quick service restaurants as a franchisee of the renowned A&W and Pizza Pizza brand. Our dedication to serving delicious, high-quality food in a fast and convenient manner has earned us a reputation for excellence in the QSR industry. We're passionate about creating dining experiences that bring people together and exceed their expectations.

Investment Philosophy:

At KAR Holdings, we firmly believe in the value of strategic investments. Our philosophy revolves around:

  • Long-Term Vision: We approach every investment with a long-term perspective. Our goal is to build enduring assets that provide value not only for our stakeholders but also for the communities we serve.
  • Sustainable Growth: Sustainability is embedded in our investment strategy. We invest in eco-friendly technologies and practices, such as energy-efficient fuel dispensers and waste reduction initiatives, to minimize our environmental footprint.
  • Community Engagement: We actively engage with the communities where we operate, striving to be responsible corporate citizens. Whether it's supporting local initiatives or creating job opportunities, we're dedicated to making a positive impact.
  • Innovation: We continuously explore innovative technologies and operational efficiencies to enhance the customer experience and drive business growth.

Why Invest with Us:

  • Proven Track Record: Our successful history in the fuel and QSR industries demonstrates our competence and reliability as an investment partner.
  • Diverse Portfolio: We offer a diverse range of investment opportunities, catering to various risk appetites and investment goals.
  • Community-Centric Approach: We believe in building strong ties with local communities, ensuring investments have a positive impact.
  • Experienced Team: Our team comprises experienced professionals with deep industry knowledge, offering unparalleled expertise in navigating the complexities of the fuel and food sectors.

Join Us: We invite prospective investors who share our vision of sustainable growth, community enrichment, and excellence in the fuel and dining sectors to partner with KAR Holdings Ltd. Together, we can drive progress, deliver superior returns, and create lasting value for all stakeholders.
