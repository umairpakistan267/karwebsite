Management Team

People Behind Our Growth

Meet the team captains who lead KAR Group’s sites, people, and operations with passion, discipline, and a commitment to excellence across Western Canada.

Our Team Captains

From finance and operations to multi‑site leadership, these captains are at the heart of KAR Group’s day‑to‑day success.

Mr. Hashim Ahmed

Team Captain

Hashim Ahmed, a Team Captain at KAR Group, embarked on his professional journey with the company immediately after graduating from the University of Alberta with a Bachelor of Commerce, majoring in Finance. His passion for the industry was ignited after a meeting with Rashid Aziz, where they discussed the exciting opportunities available.

Since joining KAR Group, Hashim has taken on diverse responsibilities, showcasing his versatility and commitment to the company’s success. He has managed multiple sites, demonstrating his operational expertise and leadership skills. Furthermore, he has played a crucial role in setting up new sites, ensuring their smooth launch and efficient operations. Hashim’s dedication to fostering a skilled and knowledgeable workforce is evident in his involvement in training new teams and Suncor dealers.

Hashim’s educational background in finance, combined with his hands‑on experience in various aspects of the business, makes him a valuable asset to KAR Group. His ability to manage multiple sites, contribute to new site setups, and train new teams highlights his adaptability and eagerness to take on challenges.

Ms. Lilia Philipov

Team Captain

Lilia Philipov is a dedicated and results‑driven Team Captain at KAR Group, with a proven track record of success in the retail industry. She holds a Business Management diploma from ICS Canada, where she gained valuable knowledge in management essentials, human resources, operations management, business planning, organizational behaviour, communications, and managing financial resources.

Lilia’s professional journey with Domo stores began over a decade ago as a gas bar attendant. Her exceptional performance was quickly recognised, leading to her promotion to Store Manager within a month, where she increased store sales by 156% in a year and maintained top service scores in Edmonton. She later became an Area Supervisor overseeing five stores.

In 2020, Lilia joined KAR Group as a Multi‑Site Manager overseeing 13 Domo stores in Edmonton. She focused on store cleanliness, organisation, shrinkage control, and ordering to boost margins and C‑store sales. During COVID‑19, her leadership kept stores operational at full capacity by prioritising local hiring and supporting her teams.

Ms. Maria Oida

Team Captain

Maria Oida, a dynamic Team Captain at KAR Group, has consistently proven her dedication and leadership throughout her impressive career journey. A graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Maria began her path at KAR Group in 2009 as a Cashier/GSA.

Her commitment to excellence and eagerness to learn led to rapid advancement. Within three years she was promoted to Store Manager and won Employee of the Year awards. She went on to train new retailers and oversee site operations, becoming Operations Manager in 2020 and later General Manager in 2022–2023.

In these leadership roles, Maria spearheaded the setup of new stores, implemented effective merchandising strategies, and introduced innovative products that significantly contributed to company growth. Her positive attitude, focus on continuous improvement, and passion for teamwork make her an inspiring leader and a key part of KAR Group’s ongoing success.
