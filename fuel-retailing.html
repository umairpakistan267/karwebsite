Fuel Retailing

Representing leading brands like Petro‑Canada, Esso, and Domo, KAR Group delivers a complete, customer‑focused fuel experience across Western Canada.

Retail Fuel Excellence

KAR Group has established a strong presence in the fuel retailing sector, representing several renowned brands, including Petro‑Canada, Esso, Domo Gasoline, and KIKIT. This strategic diversification of fuel brands provides customers with a range of options to suit their preferences and needs.

At KAR Group’s fuel stations, the emphasis goes beyond merely providing fuel. The company is committed to offering a comprehensive and customer‑centric experience, with clean and well‑maintained facilities, efficient and friendly service, and a focus on environmental sustainability.

Many locations incorporate modern features such as car wash services, convenience stores, and electric vehicle charging stations, catering to a wide spectrum of customer requirements. KAR Group also actively explores and implements eco‑friendly initiatives, including biofuels and energy‑efficient technologies, helping reduce its carbon footprint and support a more sustainable future.
