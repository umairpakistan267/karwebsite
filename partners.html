Our Trusted Partners

Building strong partnerships with industry leaders to deliver exceptional value and service across Western Canada.

Introduce Our Partners

KAR Group's core businesses represent a diverse and dynamic portfolio that has evolved and expanded over the years. At its foundation, the company is deeply involved in the fuel retailing sector, proudly representing well-known industry giants such as Petro Canada, Esso, Domo Gasoline, and Kikit. Through these partnerships, KAR Group ensures that customers across Western Canada have access to top-tier fuel products, coupled with exceptional service and convenience.

In addition to fuel retailing, KAR Group has successfully ventured into the world of quick-service dining, proudly operating as a franchisee of Pizza Pizza. This expansion into the fast-food industry demonstrates the company's commitment to diversification while providing customers with delicious and convenient dining options.

Furthermore, KAR Group has made significant inroads into the construction of gas stations, becoming a key player in this crucial infrastructure sector. Their expertise in building modern, efficient, and environmentally responsible refuelling facilities has not only contributed to the company's growth but has also enhanced the accessibility and sustainability of fuel services in Western Canada.

Last but not least, KAR Group has ventured into the merchandising of third-party convenience stores, further solidifying its presence in the retail sector. This strategic move not only expands the company's product offerings but also strengthens its position as a versatile and customer-centric organization.

Through its diverse array of core businesses, KAR Group showcases its adaptability, commitment to excellence, and determination to meet the evolving needs of the communities it serves in Western Canada. This comprehensive approach allows the company to provide a holistic and exceptional experience to customers while contributing to the vitality of the regions in which it operates.
